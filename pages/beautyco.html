How to Use

The Ritual

I

Cleanse

Begin with a gentle cleanse. Remove the day's impurities and prepare your skin to receive the full benefits of what follows.

II

Tone with Essence

Press the Dewy Essence gently into skin with your palms. This primes the skin and boosts absorption of the serum.

III

Apply Gold Elixir

3–4 drops of the Gold Elixir Serum, warmed between your palms, then pressed gently across the face and neck.

Seal & Protect

Finish with Lumière SPF in the morning, or Midnight Repair Cream in the evening. Seal in all the goodness.
