FAQ

Common Questions

How long does a typical project take? +
A landing page typically takes 1–2 weeks, a full business website 3–6 weeks, and e-commerce projects 6–10 weeks. Timeline depends on complexity, content readiness, and revision rounds. We'll give you a precise timeline during discovery.
Do you offer post-launch support? +
Yes! Every plan includes post-launch support. Starter comes with 1 month, Pro with 3 months, and Enterprise with 12 months. We also offer monthly maintenance retainers for ongoing updates.
What technologies do you use? +
We use modern, battle-tested technologies: React/Next.js for web apps, HTML/CSS/JS for simpler sites, Node.js for backends, Supabase or PostgreSQL for databases, and Stripe for payments.
How many revisions are included? +
Starter includes 2 revision rounds, Pro includes 4, and Enterprise is unlimited. We structure revisions after the design phase so you have full control before development begins.
Do you provide hosting and domain setup? +
We assist with hosting setup on Vercel, Netlify, or AWS, and can manage DNS/domain configuration. The hosting account remains yours to control and pay for directly.
